What type of support are you looking for?

It's Professional.

All counselors are qualified and experienced professionals. Mindscape allows you to connect with them in a safe and convenient online environment.

It's Free.

Access counseling services and support at no cost. Mental health support doesn't have to be expensive for Chuka University students.

It's Convenient.

Do it at your own time and at your own pace. Communicate with your counselor as often as you want and whenever you feel it's needed.

It's Effective.

Thousands of students have benefited from mental health support. With Mindscape, you can switch counselors at any point if you don't feel you are getting enough benefit.
